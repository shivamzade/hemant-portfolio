Automotive Engineering Leader | 18+ Years of Excellence

With nearly two decades of hands-on experience in vehicle design and development, I specialize in body-in-white (BIW) structures, closures, and complete automotive systems. My expertise spans the entire product lifecycle—from concept to production—across multiple vehicle platforms including traditional and electric vehicles.

I bring deep knowledge in global regulatory compliance (FMVSS, IIHS, EEC), advanced materials engineering, and EV battery integration. As a team leader, I thrive in high-pressure environments, turning complex challenges into successful projects while keeping teams motivated and focused on excellence.

My Professional Journey

From concept to production across premium EV, heritage restoration, and mass-market vehicles

Current Role at Rivian

Currently, I work at Rivian in Southern California on the electrical hardware team, focusing on mechanical integration and engineering. This role allows me to work at the cutting edge of electric vehicle technology, integrating advanced sensor systems, cameras, and radars across multiple platforms including R1S, R1T, R2, R3, and the Rivian/Amazon delivery van.

Leadership at Revology Cars

Prior to Rivian, I led the body engineering team at Revology Cars. This unique role involved setting product features to be introduced in the market, detailed project scheduling with delivery milestones, and resource planning. I developed closure mechanisms for luxury retro-mod vehicles.

These projects required marrying modern engineering practices with the authentic design language of 1960s-era vehicles—a unique challenge that demanded creativity and precision. Successfully introduced contemporary engineering practices into a legacy rework environment.

Premium EV Development at Faraday Future

During my time at Faraday Future, I was deeply involved in the development of a luxury EV. I learned to maximize limited resources while delivering high-quality results under tight timelines. I helped define engineering strategies, make build-versus-buy decisions, and drive quality and cost improvements through cross-functional collaboration.

I've been directly responsible for the engineering side of closures builds, including:

  • Part sourcing and supplier management
  • Jig/fixture design and prototyping
  • Dimensional control and testing
  • Frontal and side impact protection design
  • Development of reliable,repeatable solutions to complex build issues

Diverse Industry Exposure

My experience with automotive, aerospace, and medical device companies through engineering services has exposed me to quite different products and their manufacturing workflows and know-how. This cross-industry perspective brings unique insights to automotive challenges.

Throughout my career, I've built relationships with various suppliers from steel and aluminum tier 1s to specialized component manufacturers, creating a robust network that enables efficient project execution.
