About Myself

With nearly two decades of hands-on experience in vehicle design and development, I specialize in body-in-white (BIW) structures, closures, and complete automotive systems. My expertise spans the entire product lifecycle—from concept to production—across multiple vehicle platforms including traditional and electric vehicles. I bring deep knowledge in global regulatory compliance (FMVSS, IIHS, EEC), advanced materials engineering, and EV battery integration. As a team leader, I thrive in high-pressure environments, turning complex challenges into successful projects while keeping teams motivated and focused on excellence.

Core Competencies

Comprehensive automotive engineering expertise spanning vehicle design, development, validation, and production launch across multiple platforms and organizations.

BIW & Closures Design

Expert in body-in-white structures, closure systems (doors, hoods, liftgates), and front-end structures. Comprehensive knowledge of materials, joining methods, and manufacturing processes.

Project Management

Full lifecycle project management from concept through production. Skilled in APQP, DVP&R, risk management, and cross-functional team coordination across global suppliers and manufacturing sites.

EV Platform Integration

Experience in battery packaging, center of gravity optimization, and variable platform architecture (VPA) for electric vehicles. Expertise in sensor and electrical hardware integration for autonomous systems.

Regulatory Compliance

Deep understanding of global automotive standards including FMVSS, IIHS, SINCAP, EEC, and EPA. Ensuring all designs meet safety, crash performance, durability, and environmental requirements.

Manufacturing & Tooling

Extensive experience in mass production, prototype builds, tooling development, and design for manufacturability (DFM). Skilled in supplier management and PPAP documentation.

Leadership & Innovation

Proven leader with MBA in Strategic Innovation and Entrepreneurship. Skilled in building high-performing teams, conflict resolution, decision-making, and driving continuous improvement in fast-paced environments.
